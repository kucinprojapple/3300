PumpFlight Ñ–s a an app that helps you with training. It provides assitance with a collection of exercises that everyone can do at home and provides a unique way of making workouts exciting! 

When you want to start a workout you need to pick one of the two options. Either pick your excersises one by one, your choosing or switch it up with exciting random flips, where you randomly get assigned an exercise to do. This way each workouts feel different and its easier to build your routine. After the timer for the excersise is done, log your reps and continue onto the next one.

Your profile is your achievement display: 
- avatar with unlockable frames,
- fun titles
- exercises completed
- minutes trained
- total reps
- favorite exercise

Unlock new titles and frames by completing achievements, this is an extra way of positive reinforment to build your healthy routine.

If you are not familiar with the exercise tap the library for quick instructions and guidance on our selection of exercises!
